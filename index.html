Multi-AZ Clustering

Leverage the 99.99% uptime SLA offered by AWS, Azure, and GCP for multi-AZ deployments. SkyCluster and FlashGrid Storage Fabric simplify clustering across availability zones. FlashGrid Read-Local™ Technology minimizes network overhead by serving read traffic from local storage devices.

Designed
for Oracle Databases

SkyCluster, SkyBase, and FlashGrid Storage Fabric integrate seamlessly with Oracle Clusterware and Oracle ASM. Extensive testing with the entire Oracle database stack ensures seamless compatibility and reliable operation.

DBA Friendly

SkyCluster, SkyBase, and FlashGrid Storage Fabric are easy to use for Oracle DBAs. Lift-and-shift your existing DBA expertise along with your existing databases. Use familiar tools and database management procedures.

Keep Your Databases Infrastructure-Agnostic

Compliance with local regulations, mergers, acquisitions, or simply lowering costs – there are multiple reasons why in future you may need to run your databases in a different public cloud or in a private data center.
